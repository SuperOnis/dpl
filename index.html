The bridge from digital to physical; copy-pasting will never be the same.

What do we do?

Our goal is to design a product which allows anybody to seamlessy print any text physically, as if it was just a simple copy-paste operation.

How do we work?

Copying

Simply use CTRL+C while your text is selected to copy, or right click and select "Copy to printer".

Symbols will be ommited.

Pasting

Extendable stamps located at the bottom of the printer allow any letters or numbers to be printed on any surface.

Font style and size can be selected when purchasing your printer.

Software

DPL's software allows you to effortlessly connect your electronic device with our printers, as by only pressing CTRL+FN+V.

Multiple printers may also be connected for different font sizes or styles.
